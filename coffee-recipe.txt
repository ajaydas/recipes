Recipe to make pour-over coffee:

1. Bring water to boil in a kettle
2. Grind coffee beans (2 teaspoon for each cup) in a coffee grinder to medium fine grounds
3. Place a funnel over a coffee pot and place a coffee filter paper inside the funnel
4. Pour the coffee grinds into the coffee filter inside the funnel
5. Slowly pour hot water from the kettle onto the coffee grinds covering all the grinds 
6. Continue pouring water taking care as bubbles form on the grinds and rise up
7. After finishing pouring required amount water, remove the funnel with the grounds and discard the grounds
8. Pour the coffee from the pot into your favorite coffee mug and enjoy!!!
